# Privacy Policy for SSTV Decoder

**Last Updated: January 2025**

## Introduction

SSTV Decoder ("we", "our", or "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how our application handles information when you use the SSTV Decoder mobile application.

## Information Collection and Use

### Audio Data
- **Microphone Access**: The app requires microphone permission to capture audio signals for SSTV decoding
- **Local Processing**: All audio data is processed locally on your device in real-time
- **No Storage**: Audio data is not saved, stored, or transmitted to any server
- **No Recording**: The app does not record or retain audio beyond the immediate processing needed for decoding

### Decoded Images
- **Local Storage**: Decoded SSTV images are only saved to your device when you explicitly choose to save them
- **User Control**: You have complete control over saving, sharing, or deleting decoded images
- **No Upload**: Images are never automatically uploaded to our servers or any third party

### Analytics and Tracking
- **No Analytics**: We do not use any analytics services
- **No Tracking**: We do not track your usage or behavior
- **No Ads**: The app contains no advertisements or ad tracking

## Data Transmission

- **No Network Transmission**: The app does not transmit any personal data, audio data, or images over the network
- **Offline Functionality**: The app works completely offline after installation
- **Internet Connection**: An internet connection is only required for initial app installation and updates

## Permissions

The app requests the following permissions:

1. **Microphone (RECORD_AUDIO)**: Required to capture audio signals for SSTV decoding
2. **Internet Access (INTERNET)**: Required to load the Progressive Web App content
3. **Wake Lock (WAKE_LOCK)**: Keeps the screen on during active decoding to prevent interruption

## Third-Party Services

This app does not integrate with any third-party services, analytics platforms, or advertising networks.

## Data Security

- All processing occurs locally on your device
- No data is transmitted to external servers
- No user accounts or authentication required
- No personal information collected

## Children's Privacy

This app does not knowingly collect any personal information from children under 13 years of age. The app does not collect personal information from users of any age.
